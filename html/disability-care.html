Our Disability Care Services

At Carnation Joy, we provide a wide range of services to support individuals with disabilities. Our team is dedicated to helping each individual achieve their goals while respecting their independence and dignity.

Personal Assistance

We offer support with daily tasks, such as dressing, grooming, and managing day-to-day activities, all tailored to your needs.

Transportation Support

Our reliable transportation services help individuals maintain independence by ensuring they get to medical appointments and community events safely.

Community Participation

We encourage involvement in social activities, helping individuals become active members of their community while building meaningful relationships.

Our Care Services Timeline

Initial Consultation

We start with a thorough assessment to understand the unique needs of each individual and plan personalized care services accordingly.

Care Planning

Our team collaborates with families to create a customized care plan that aligns with the individual's goals and requirements.

Service Commencement

Once the plan is finalized, our qualified care professionals start delivering services with compassion and dedication.

Ongoing Support

We provide continuous monitoring, regular check-ins, and adjustments to ensure the individual’s evolving needs are met effectively.

Review and Feedback

We collect feedback from families and individuals to improve our services and ensure satisfaction.

Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you provide for disability care?

We offer a variety of services including personal assistance, transportation support, and community participation to help individuals with disabilities lead fulfilling lives.

How do I apply for disability support?

You can apply by filling out the contact form on our website or giving us a call. We will guide you through the process to ensure you receive the support you need.

Are your services covered by NDIS?

Yes, we are a registered NDIS provider, and our services are covered under the National Disability Insurance Scheme.
