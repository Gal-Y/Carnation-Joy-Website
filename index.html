Services

with Carnation Joy

Daily Living Assistance

Help with personal hygiene, dressing, and grooming to ensure participants can manage their day-to-day activities comfortably.

Meal Preparation and Feeding

Preparing nutritious meals and assisting with feeding to promote health and well-being.

Mobility Support

Assistance with moving around the home or community, ensuring participants can stay active and engaged.

Housekeeping

Cleaning, laundry, and general household maintenance to create a safe and comfortable living environment.

Social Activities

Support to attend community events, social gatherings, and recreational activities, fostering social connections and engagement.

Transport Assistance

Help with transportation to appointments, activities, and social engagements, ensuring participants can access necessary services and activities.

Occupational Therapy

Improving daily living and working skills to enhance independence and quality of life.

Respite Care

Providing temporary care to give regular carers a break, ensuring they can rest and recharge while participants continue to receive quality care.
